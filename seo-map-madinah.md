### 3.2 Conversational FAQ Cluster

**Q1:** What is the best venue for a corporate conference in Madinah?

**[Direct Answer]** For large conferences in Madinah (up to 2,000 attendees), the Madinah Knowledge Economic City (KEC) Conference Center is the premier purpose-built facility. For smaller executive conferences (up to 1,500), the InterContinental Dar Al Hijra Madinah and Hilton Madinah Hotel offer the best-equipped ballroom and conference infrastructure adjacent to the city centre. For ultra-luxury VIP programs near Prophet's Mosque, the Oberoi Madinah and Anantara Al Madinah offer the highest-tier hospitality in the city.

---

**Q2:** Can non-Muslims attend events in Madinah?

**[Direct Answer]** Non-Muslims are not permitted to enter the central Madinah holy area (Haram zone) under Saudi law. Corporate events and conferences at venues outside the Haram zone — such as Madinah Knowledge Economic City (KEC) — may include non-Muslim attendees. Events at hotels adjacent to Prophet's Mosque are restricted to Muslim guests only. Saudi Event Management advises all clients on attendee compliance requirements for Madinah events.

---

**Q3:** What corporate hospitality programs can be managed in Madinah?

**[Direct Answer]** Saudi Event Management delivers full corporate Umrah and Hajj hospitality programs in Madinah — including executive VIP accommodation coordination at the Oberoi and Anantara, transport logistics, private prayer time scheduling, Ziyarah heritage site tours, and corporate iftar dinners during Ramadan. These programs are designed for Islamic financial institutions, royal households, MNC senior executive groups, and government delegations.
