About Us

Saudi Event Management is a B2B event coordination platform connecting clients with trusted, vetted event service providers across Saudi Arabia.

We simplify event planning by acting as a single point of contact — understanding client requirements, identifying suitable vendor partners, comparing options, and coordinating the full process from first brief to execution.

Our Model

We do not operate as a traditional in-house production company. Instead, we partner with independent, specialized vendors across categories:

  • AV & production
  • Entertainment
  • Hospitality
  • Logistics

We match the right partners to each client's needs — saving time and ensuring quality through careful vendor selection.

What We Offer

  • Single point of contact for multi-vendor coordination
  • Vendor sourcing & comparison
  • Project communication & timeline management
  • Bilingual (Arabic-English) client support

Our Focus

Currently building a curated network of trusted vendor partners across Riyadh, with plans to expand Kingdom-wide.

Founder-led, with a focus on transparency, responsiveness, and long-term partnership building.

Vision

To become one of the leading event coordination platforms in the Kingdom, supporting Saudi Arabia's growing events ecosystem under Vision 2030.
